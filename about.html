About Us

Welcome to Kuriftu, where luxury meets comfort. Our hotel has been providing exceptional hospitality for 9 years.

At Kuriftu, we are committed to making your stay memorable. Our 5 star facilities, attentive staff, and prime location make us the ideal choice for both business and leisure travelers.

Our Story

Located on the northen tip of Bishoftu encompassing the smallest of the four lakes lies our flagship resort, Kuriftu Resort & Spa Bishoftu. Born from Mr. Tadiwos's vision to create luxury resort destinations in neighboring cities of the capital, this resort made its mark in 2007 as the first 5-star resort in the country. With just 14 rooms and a lakefront restaurant at the time, Mr. Tadiwos made his offical debut by setting the standard for luxury and exquisite service, attracting guests from around the world to this unique establishment.
